About us

Our game consists of Heroes and Warriors, who are in a constant struggle for survival against the Demon Hordes that threaten to overrun the earth.

Build your team and battle the Demons to earn rewards, upgrade your characters, and earn valuable items in our strategy game that contains thousands of potential outcomes.

Players can earn by

  • Defeating Demon Hordes to earn rewards.
  • Selling/trasing NFTs in the marketplace.
  • Farming tokens.
  • Staking tokens.
