About us

About us

Our game consists of Heroes and Warriors, who are in a constant struggle for survival against the Demon Hordes that threaten to overrun the earth.

Build your team and battle the Demons to earn rewards, upgrade your characters, and earn valuable items in our strategy game that contains thousands of potential outcomes.

Players can earn by

  • Defeating Demon Hordes to earn rewards.
  • Selling/trasing NFTs in the marketplace.
  • Farming tokens.
  • Staking tokens.

The Lore

The Lore

In the aftermath of relentless solitary battles, a fragile peace settled upon the war-weary world. The scars of past conflicts were etched into the remnants of shattered landscapes and reflected in the haunted gazes of survivors.

As time passed, however, a new threat silently crept into the shadows—a danger not born of external conflicts, but of internal strife fueled by the unquenchable desire for power and control. Amidst this delicate peace, preeminent race leader found themselves caught in the undertow of a resurging storm.

Having weathered the storms of the past, they had hoped for an era of reconstruction and unity. Yet, the shadows of power supremacy cast a pall over the world’s newfound stability. Could this mark the beginning of an epic all-out-war? Join us as the story hurtles towards a thrilling pinnacle, where uncertainty ignites the flames of conflict, and the fate of peace hangs in the balance!
